You can add any number of Steps.

<Steps>
	<Step title='First Step'>
		These are instructions or content that only pertain to the first step.
	</Step>
	<Step title='Second Step'>
		These are instructions or content that only pertain to the second step.
	</Step>
	<Step title='Third Step'>
		These are instructions or content that only pertain to the third step.
	</Step>
</Steps>

## Variants

Steps have two variants: `subtle` and `outline` to distinugish content such as the current step or simply customizing the look and feel. The `variant` prop can be set on both the `Steps` component and individual `Step` components.

<Steps variant='subtle'>
	<Step title='First Step'>
		These are instructions or content that only pertain to the first step.
	</Step>
	<Step title='Second Step'>
		These are instructions or content that only pertain to the second step.
	</Step>
</Steps>

<Steps>
	<Step title='First Step' variant='outline'>
		These are instructions or content that only pertain to the first step.
	</Step>
	<Step title='Second Step' variant='outline'>
		These are instructions or content that only pertain to the second step.
	</Step>
</Steps>

</RequestExample>

## Steps Props

<ResponseField name='children' type='ReactElement<StepProps>[]' required>
	A list of `Step` components.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name='variant' type="string">
	The styling variant of the steps. One of `subtle` or `outline`.
</ResponseField>

## Individual Step Props

<ResponseField name='icon' type='string or svg'>
	A [Font Awesome icon](https://fontawesome.com/icons) or SVG code in `icon={}`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name='iconType' type='string'>
	One of `regular`, `solid`, `light`, `thin`, `sharp-solid`, `duotone`, `brands`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name='title' type='string'>
	The title is the primary text for the step and shows up next to the indicator.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name='stepNumber' type='number'>
	The number of the step.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name='children' type='string | ReactNode' required>
	The content of a step either as plain text, or components.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name='variant' type="string">
	The styling variant of the steps. The styling variant of the steps. One of `subtle` or `outline`.
</ResponseField>
